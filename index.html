About the Practice

Meet Dr Rirhandzu Maluleke

A dedicated general practitioner committed to your long-term health and wellbeing.

Dr Rirhandzu Maluleke

MBChB (SMU)

PR No: 1317512

RM Health was founded on a simple belief: every patient deserves compassionate, evidence-based care.

Based at Local Choice Pharmacy Mahumani in Lowveld Mall, Hazyview, Dr Maluleke provides comprehensive primary healthcare to individuals and families across the Mpumalanga lowveld region.

With a holistic approach to medicine, Dr Maluleke takes time to understand each patient's unique circumstances — ensuring diagnoses are thorough, treatment plans are personalised, and follow-up care is consistent.
